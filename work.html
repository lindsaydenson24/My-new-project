Work

What is the start of my work history?
I worked as an appointment secretary at an Orthopedic clinic after I graduated from college.
What led me to the Navy?
I realized that I wanted to be a part of something bigger. I come from a Military family and I wanted to follow suit. I love to travel and see new places so that was a plus when joining.
What are my pursuits after the Navy?
After the Navy I knew that I had a very strong in computers. On the ship that I was stationed on, I got to know my shipmates that worked in Information Technology and saw what they worked on which sparked an interest in me. I did some research on IT and cybersecurity and realized that I have an interest in cyber forensics where I can use my skills of protection and data recovery to help law enforcement.
